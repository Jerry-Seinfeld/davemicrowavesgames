Strange Terror From The Deep:

You survived the terrors of space, it took a few years to recover from that experience
and now you're back on your feet, ready to get back to work.
However, your luck just can't get any worse.
The creature you were transporting on your submersible has broken free from captivity
and killed off most of your crew. All that's left is you, Alice, Dr. Salmon, Commander Nathan
and The Captain. Now only one question remains.
Are you a bad enough dude to escape the terrors from the deep?
Explore a large and all new derelict submersible, featuring 9 endings, a bunch of gadgets, tools,
objectives and 25 brand new monsters, each with their own special abilities, strengths and weaknesses!
Featuring special guest monsters such as, Baldi (legally added in and approved by Basicly Games LLC),
an old friend from Strange Terror From Beyond The Stars, Nosferatu and more!
And if you ever feel like the terrors that lurk in the deep are too easy to handle,
then get ready for all new modes such as Hardcore Mode, a challenge only veterans can accomplish,
Monochrome Mode, a throwback to the original and Custom Mode, select whichever monster you wish to go up against!

Strange Terror From Beyond The Stars:

There were twenty crew members at the beginning of the galactic expedition of 1967,
but only five remain in the aftermath of the strange terror...
Witness a truely horrifying sci-fi game, with a 60s aesthetic.
Each time you play, you'll go up against one of seventeen monsters,
each with their own special abilities. Including special guests from iconic movies and random monstrosities!
This game was directed by none other than Dave Microwaves Games!
Featuring voice actors such as JayskiBean,
who plays the brave soul who is desperately trying to escape the ship.
NathanBlakeGames, who plays the pilot, warning the player of impending doom.
And TheGameSalmon, who plays the role of the General Maintenance Worker stuck in a closet...
Will you be able to survive playing through this entire film- erm, game?

Wails For Freedom:

Wails For Freedom is an Outlast inspired horror game
about a man attempting to escape the malevolent presence of ghosts who haunt the asylum.
You must traverse the asylum on your wheelchair
and find fuses to activate the front door to escape. This is not an easy task,
your wheelchair has limited mobility and makes turns slow and difficult.
Will insanity and frustration consume you,
or will you be able to find a way out and escape this nightmare?
